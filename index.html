Key Features of Spanish Water Dogs

  • They have a distinct wooly coat
  • They are highly intelligent
  • They are the cutest dogs ever
  • They are a versatile breed that is able to perform many tasks including
    • Herding
    • fishing
    • retrieving, especially from water
